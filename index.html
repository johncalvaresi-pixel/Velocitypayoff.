How to Use the Velocity Debt Payoff Calculator

Achieving financial freedom requires a clear execution strategy and accurate data. This tool is engineered to help you map out your exact debt free timeline using advanced amortization formulas. By strategically managing your cash flow and optimizing extra payments, you can outsmart high-interest credit cards and eliminate principal balances rapidly.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  • Net Pay & Expenses: Enter your total monthly take-home income and basic living expenses at the top to track your available monthly cash flow.
  • Debt Name: Clearly label your accounts (e.g., Chase Card, Discover Card, Citi Card) so you can track your targets individually.
  • Balance: Input the current total principal balance remaining on each specific account.
  • APR (%): Enter the annual percentage rate charged by the lender to ensure interest calculations are precise.
  • Minimum Payment vs. Extra Payment: Enter your mandatory minimum monthly obligation, then use the "Extra Pmt" box to see how accelerating your cash flow shrinks your payoff timeline.

Understanding the Velocity Banking Strategy

Velocity Banking is a powerful financial tool focused on maximizing cash efficiency to eliminate liabilities. Instead of letting your hard-earned income sit idle in a standard account, the velocity method utilizes strategic cash flow sweeps to reduce high-interest principal balances immediately. This minimizes the compounding effect of interest over time, potentially saving you thousands of dollars in hidden lending fees.
